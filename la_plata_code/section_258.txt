Sec. 2-691.121. Creation
I.
That there is hereby created the Pine River Library District
Board
of Trustees (PRLDB) to be comprised of seven members appointed by the Board of
County
Commissioners
.
II.
Members of the board of trustees may be removed by the Board of County Commissioners, based upon a showing of good cause as defined in, but not limited to, the bylaws of the library district.
(
Res. No. 2010-08, § 1, 02/09/2010; Res. No. 2023-08, § 42-94, 02/21/2023
)
Effective on: 2/21/2023
Sec. 2-691.122. Term; vacancies; qualifications
I.
Bylaws adopted by the PRLDB shall govern the length and number of terms of the members of the board of trustees, pursuant to C.R.S. § 24-90-108(3)(a), as amended.
II.
Unless removed for good cause, members of the PRLDB shall serve until their successors are appointed.
III.
Vacancies shall be filled by special appointment made by the
board of county commissioners
. Such appointment shall be to fill the unexpired term of the vacant member's seat.
IV.
All members must be residents within the legal service area of the Pine River Library District.
V.
The board of county commissioners authorizes the PRLDB to recommend new trustees for consideration by the
board
of
county
commissioners
for appointment.
(
Res. No. 2010-08, § 1, 02/09/2010; Res. No. 2023-08, § 42-94, 02/21/2023
)
Effective on: 2/21/2023
Sec. 2-691.123. Quorum
The presence of a majority of the PRLDB shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business.
(
Res. No. 2010-08, § 1, 02/09/2010; Res. No. 2023-08, § 42-94, 02/21/2023
)
Effective on: 2/21/2023
Sec. 2-691.124. Powers and duties; compensation
I.
PRLDB
shall
have the powers and duties as provided in C.R.S. § 24-90-109.
II.
PRLDB members shall serve without compensation, although necessary traveling and subsistence expenses actually incurred
may
be paid from the public library fund, in accordance with the duly adopted bylaws of the PRLDB.
(
Res. No. 2010-08, § 1, 02/09/2010; Res. No. 2023-08, § 42-94, 02/21/2023
)
Effective on: 2/21/2023
