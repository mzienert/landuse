Sec. 58-57. Advisory commission.
The
BOCC
has appointed the
advisory commission
from
landowners
and operators living within the
county
boundaries as provided in sections 2-646 et seq., and the advisory commission
shall
have all of the authority and powers set forth therein as well as all of the authority and powers set forth in C.R.S. § 35-5.5-107. The BOCC shall be entitled to appoint ex-officio members, as in their sole discretion they shall determine.
