DIVISION 6. TRANSPORT PERMIT OPERATING REQUIREMENTS
Sec. 42-412. Minimum distances.
A minimum distance of one-half mile
shall
be maintained at all times between extra-legal
vehicles
or load, except when passing or when otherwise authorized under a
special permit
. One extra-legal vehicle or load
may
pass another extra-legal vehicle or load only under the following conditions:
There must be no other traffic in the immediate vicinity on the
county
highway; and
The vehicles or loads involved must communicate by radio concerning the pass prior to making the passing maneuver.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(4-7), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
