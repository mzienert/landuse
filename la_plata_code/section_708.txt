DIVISION 1. GENERALLY
Sec. 46-31. Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article,
shall
have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
Dead animal
m
eans any dead animal or all or part of any animal carcass.
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Designated facility
mean
s the county-owned waste transfer stations located in Bayfield and Marvel, Colorado, and the City of Durango-owned waste transfer station located in Durango, Colorado, and any legally operated landfill and/or transfer station.
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Dumping
mea
ns, but is not limited to, throwing, depositing, dropping, placing, leaving, discarding and scattering.
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Hauler
mean
s any person engaged in the business of collection, transportation or delivery of rubbish to a lawfully permitted solid waste disposal facility.
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Public or private property
means, but is not limited to, any road or highway and its right-of-way; any roadside, alley, ditch, glade or arroyo; any b
ody of water or watercourse, including frozen areas or shores or beaches thereof; any park, playground, building, refuge, conservation or recreation area; any residential, farm, or ranch properties; timberlands; and approved waste containers or receptacles, unless the person dumping is the owner or tenant of the containers or receptacles.
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Rubbish
means, but
is not limited to, decomposed and nondecomposed solid waste, refuse, garbage, trash, debris, litter, foreign substances (either solid or liquid), junk, ash, industrial waste, discarded material, recyclable material, banned materials, combustible trash, wastepaper, construction or demolition debris, yard waste, tires, housewares, furniture, appliances, machinery, vehicles or portions thereof, or any petroleum products.
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Tipping fee
means the charge p
ayable to the county or the City of Durango for disposal of rubbish at the designated facilities.
(Ord. No. 1997-2, § 1, 9-8-1997)
