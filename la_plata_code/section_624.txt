DIVISION 2. EXEMPTIONS
Sec. 42-213. Special mobile equipment exemption (SMEE).
Special
mobile
equipment
exemptions
shall
be recognized in the
county
, provided that proof of an exemption certificate issued by the state is made for the specific
vehicle
. All state requirements for moving special mobile equipment shall be applicable to any and all moves within the county.
A copy of the exemption certificate shall be carried in the vehicle or combination of vehicles to which it refers and shall be open to inspection by any police officer or authorized
agent
of any authority granting such exemption letter, or by the
department
. A copy of the exemption certificate shall be provided to the
permit officer
.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(8-1), (8-2), 9-8-1997)
