Sec. 46-56. Disposal of rubbish.
The
owner
or occupant of each
residential
,
agricultural
, commercial, institutional and industrial property within the unincorporated areas of the
county
shall
be responsible for securing an approved method of collection for
rubbish
or
dead animals
generated on their
premises
. Approved methods shall be limited to:
Arrangements for transport by a commercial
hauler
;
Transport and proper disposal of a
person
's own rubbish to a
designated facility
; or
Transport and proper disposal of dead animals to a designated facility, or proper disposal as defined in C.R.S. § 25-1-612.
It shall be unlawful for any person to allow, cause or
permit
the
dumping
of rubbish or dead animals upon any private or public lands within the unincorporated area of the county, except in accordance with the provisions of this article.
No owner or occupant of a residential or commercial establishment shall permit the accumulation of rubbish or dead animals upon any premises for a period in excess of 30 days.
(Ord. No. 1997-2, § 2, 9-8-1997)
