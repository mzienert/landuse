ARTICLE III. MAINTENANCE
FOOTNOTE(S):
Editor's note—
Res. No. 2002-13, §§ 1—5, adopted March 11, 2002, amended art. III in its entirety to read as herein set out. Formerly, art. III pertained to similar subject matter and derived from Res. No. 1994-4, adopted January 4, 1994.
Sec. 42-92. Criteria and maintenance level for primary roads.
Primary roads
within the
county
's road system
shall
meet the following criteria:
Be legally open to public travel by ordinary motor
vehicles
at all times;
Be usable by the public at all times except during
adverse weather conditions
; and
Be maintained by work of county maintenance crews or crews of other statutory entities or governmental agencies within the state that are authorized to engage in highway road or street maintenance or
improvement
performed on a continual basis.
(Res. No. 2002-13, § 2, 3-11-2002)
Effective on: 3/11/2002
