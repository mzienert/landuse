DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-306. Required.
Pursuant to the authority conferred by C.R.S. §§ 42-4-510 and 42-4-511, as amended, the
owner or operator
of all trucks, other motor
vehicles
, vehicles, or combination of trucks or motor vehicles which exceed the weight and/or size restrictions set forth in division 4 of this article, which are to be operated, driven or moved upon any
county roads
or highways, for which no specific weight or size limits have been set as provided in appendices B or C to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which are on file in the clerk and recorder's office, or as provided in section 42-384 or 42-385, are required to apply for and receive the appropriate
permit
authorizing such activity from the
county
road and bridge
department
, prior to transiting, in any manner, on any county road, highway or bridge.
It
shall
be unlawful to operate a vehicle on any county road or bridge when such vehicle exceeds the limits set forth in division 4 of this article, without a
transport permit
issued by the department.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(2-9), 9-8-1997)
