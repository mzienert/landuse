DIVISION 25. SOUTHWEST (REGION 9) WORKFORCE BOARD
Sec. 2-690. Officers; meetings.
I.
The southwest (region 9) workforce
board
shall
elect from its membership a chair and any other officers they deem appropriate for the accomplishment of their tasks. The chair must be a representative of the private business sector.
II.
Meetings of the southwest (region 9) workforce board shall be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the Colorado Sunshine Act. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the southwest (region 9) workforce board shall be posted at the La Plata
County
Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado and at any other locations requested by the governing bodies of the other counties represented on the southwest (region 9) workforce board. The southwest (region 9) workforce board shall meet as often as necessary to fulfill its responsibilities. The quorum needed to transact business must include a majority of the private business sector representatives.
(Res. No. 1999-8, §§ 6, 7, 2-22-99)
Effective on: 10/25/2016
