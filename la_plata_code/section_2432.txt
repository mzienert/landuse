Chapter 18 BUILDINGS AND BUILDING REGULATIONS
Article III. Building Codes and standards: Amendments, additions and deletions to the international codes
Sec. 18-44 Adoption of the 2015 International Energy Conservation Code.
The volumes, parts, chapters, and appendices of the
IECC
, as published by the International Code Council, together with additions and deletions as set forth in this section, are hereby adopted pursuant to C.R.S. § 30-28-201(1).
(
Res. No. 2017-33, § 18-44, 10/10/2017; Res. No. 2023-15, § 18-36, 06/06/2023
)
