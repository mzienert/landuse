Sec. 58-65. Recoverable costs/method of collection—Management.
If the
BOCC
compels and provides for the
management
of
noxious weeds
pursuant to the provisions of C.R.S. § 35-5-109, the BOCC is entitled to assess the whole cost thereof, including up to 20 percent for inspection and other incidental costs in connection therewith, upon the
lot
or tract of land where the noxious weeds are located.
Such assessment
shall
be a lien against each lot or tract of land until paid and shall have priority over all other liens except general taxes and prior special assessments.
Such assessment
may
be certified to the
county
treasurer for the collection of taxes.
Any funds collected shall be deposited in the BOCC's
weed
fund or any similar fund.
(Res. No. 2006-43, § 1(Annex A), 11-27-2006)
Effective on: 11/27/2006
