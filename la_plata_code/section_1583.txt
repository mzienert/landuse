ARTICLE II. DIRECTIONAL SIGNS AND BUSINESS PLAQUES
Sec. 98-14. Limited rights.
Granting of the
directional sign
and
business plaque
permit
conveys no right, title or interest to the
permittees
in the highway rights-of-way or in the directional sign and business plaque other than permission to
use
and occupy such location for directional sign purposes subject to these regulations. Directional signs and plaques
shall
remain the exclusive property of the
county
.
(Res. No. 2011-16, § 1(Exh. A), 6-14-2011)
Effective on: 6/14/2011
