DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-310. Annual fleet transport permit.
Annual fleet
transport permits
for overlength are available only for public
utility
vehicles
or loads. Annual fleet
permits
for overweight are available only for longer vehicle combinations. The
maximum limits
for length and weight of such vehicles under such permits
shall
not exceed the following:
Public utility vehicles or loads:
One hundred twenty feet in length for all nonmountainous, two-lane
roadways
.
One hundred ten feet in length for all mountainous, two-lane roadways.
Longer vehicle combinations: The axle and gross vehicle weight as described in the
bridge weight limit map
, as provided in appendix B to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which is on file in the clerk and recorder's office.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(3-6), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
