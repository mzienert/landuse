DIVISION 20. SOLID WASTE ADVISORY COMMITTEE
FOOTNOTE(S):
Cross reference—
Solid waste, ch. 46.
State Law reference—
Solid waste disposal
sites
, C.R.S. § 30-20-101 et seq.
Sec. 2-596. Officers; meetings; quorum.
I.
The solid waste advisory committee members
shall
elect from their membership a chair, vice-chair and any other officers they deem appropriate for the accomplishment of their task. Meetings of the solid waste advisory committee shall be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the Colorado Sunshine Act, C.R.S. § 24-6-401 et seq. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the solid waste advisory committee shall be posted at the La Plata
County
Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado. The solid waste advisory committee shall meet as often as necessary to fulfill its responsibilities.
II.
The presence of a majority of the solid waste advisory committee shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business.
(Res. No. 1994-12, §§ 4, 5, 2-1-1994; Res. No. 1997-12, §§ 7, 8, 2-10-1997)
