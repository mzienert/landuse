DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-241. Suspension or revocation.
The
county
road and bridge
department
may
suspend or revoke an annual
permit
, fleet permit, single trip permit or
special permit
. Grounds for suspension or revocation may include, but
shall
not be limited to, the following circumstances or occurrences:
Failure to comply with the terms and conditions of a permit or this article.
Discovery by the department, sheriff or any other local issuing authority of previously undetected false statements made by the
applicant
on an application for a permit.
Failure to pay applicable ad valorem taxes prior to movement of a used
mobile home
.
Falsification or misrepresentation of an
emergency
situation, in order to obtain oral authorization to move a permitted load.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(9-3), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
