DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-271. Required.
Pursuant to the authority conferred by C.R.S. §§ 42-4-510 and 42-4-511, as amended, the
owner or operator
of all
vehicles
, trucks, other motor vehicles or combinations of trucks, vehicles and motor vehicles which exceed the specific weight limits for specific
county roads
, highways and bridges as set forth in appendix B to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which is on file in the clerk and recorder's office, or where any portion of the proposed route
shall
be upon the prohibited overweight route list as set forth in appendix C to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which is on file in the clerk and recorder's office, or adopted as set forth in division 5 of this article, are required to apply for and receive a
special permit
from the
county
road and bridge
department
prior to transiting, in any manner, on any such county road, highway or bridge. Conditions
may
be attached to special permits as provided in this article. Where applicable, a special permit shall be required in
addition
to a
transport permit
.
It shall be unlawful to operate a vehicle on any county road or bridge when such vehicle exceeds the limits set forth in the
bridge weight limit map
, as set forth in appendix B to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which is on file in the clerk and recorder's office or on the prohibited overweight route list, as set forth in appendix C to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which is on file in the clerk and recorder's office without a special permit issued by the department, or where such road or bridge has been posted with a specific weight limit as provided in sections 42-384 or
42-385.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(2-10), 9-8-1997)
