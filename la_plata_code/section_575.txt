Sec. 42-94. Road maintenance map.
Editor's note—
Res. No. 2023-06, adopted February 21, 2023, states the following: "the execution and administration of provisions of Section 42-94 of the
Code
of La Plata County, Colorado, related to acceptance of additional roads or requests for increased levels of maintenance to current roads, are hereby suspended until December 31, 2025. The County Road Maintenance Map prepared by
staff
and presented at the public hearing on February 21, 2023, and approved by the Board on the same day, shall continue to serve as the County's road maintenance map until such time as another map is adopted by the Board."
Res. No. 2020-09, § 42-94, adopted February 11, 2020, states the following: "the execution and administration of Section 42-94 of the
Code
of La Plata County, Colorado, related to acceptance of additional roads or requests for increased levels of maintenance to current roads are hereby suspended until December 31, 2022. The County Road Maintenance Map prepared by
staff
and presented at the public hearing on February 11, 2020, and approved by the Board on the same day shall continue to serve as the County's road maintenance map until such time as another map is adopted by the Board."
Res. No. 2014-10, §§ 1, 2, adopted January 28, 2014, states the following: "the execution and administration of Section 42-94 of the
Code
of La Plata County, Colorado is hereby suspended until December 31, 2016. The County Road Maintenance Map prepared by
staff
and presented at the public hearing on January 28, 2014, and approved by the Board on the same day shall continue to serve as the County's road maintenance map until such time as another map is adopted by the Board."
(
Res. No. 2002-13, § 4, 03/11/2002; Res. No. 2020-09, § 42-94, 02/11/2020; Res. No. 2023-08, § 42-94, 02/21/2023
)
The
board of county commissioners
shall
cause a road maintenance map to be prepared showing each road in the
county
's primary and secondary system as of the date of the map and the corresponding level of maintenance of each road. At least once a year, the
board
shall cause notice of its intent to consider, revise and adopt the road maintenance map to be given, which notice shall specify the time and place at which all interested
persons
will be heard. Such notice of intention shall be published once a week for at least two successive weeks preceding the date of such hearing in a newspaper of general circulation in the county. After such hearing, the board of county commissioners shall adopt the road maintenance map, with any changes or revisions deemed by it to be advisable. Such map shall establish the maintenance level of each road for the year. Such map shall not constitute the official map of the road system of the county.
Effective on: 2/21/2023
