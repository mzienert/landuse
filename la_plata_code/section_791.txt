Sec. 54-31. Definitions.
The following words, terms and phrases, when used in this article,
shall
have the meanings ascribed to them in this section, except where the context clearly indicates a different meaning:
County roads
means all public highways on the county road system as shown on the map submitted to the state department of transportation pursuant to C.R.S. § 43-2-120.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Emergency stopping
or parking only means when official signs are erected, giving notice thereof, no person shall stop, stand or park a vehicle on the shoulder of any highway or any other facility so marked except in case of an emergency involving the vehicle or its occupants.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Loading zone
means a designated space reserved for the exclusive use of vehicles during the loading or unloading of passengers or property.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Park
or parking means the standing of a vehicle, whether occupied or not, otherwise than very briefly for the purpose of and while engaged in loading or unloading property or passengers.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Snow route
means roads designated with official snow route signs, on which vehicles shall not be parked so as to impede snow removal operations which are imminent or in progress.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Stand
or standing means the halting of a vehicle, whether occupied or not, other than momentarily for the purpose of and while actually engaged in receiving or discharging passengers.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Stop
or stopping means, when prohibited, any halting, even momentarily, of a vehicle, whether occupied or not, except when necessary to avoid conflict with other traffic or in compliance with the directions of a police officer or official traffic control device.
Effective on: 4/22/2014
Vehicle
means any device which is capable of moving itself, or being moved, from place to place upon wheels or endless tracks. Such term shall include any farm tractor or any implement of husbandry designed primarily or exclusively for use and used in agricultural operations, and such term shall not mean or include any device if moved by muscular power, or moved exclusively over stationary rails or tracks, or designed to move primarily through the air.
(Ord. No. 2014-1, § 3(Exh. A), 4-22-2014)

Sec. 54-33. Disposition of fines and forfeitures.
All fines and forfeitures for
violations
of the provisions of this article
shall
be paid into the treasury of the
county
upon payment of such fines and forfeitures.
All fees for the storage of impounded
vehicles
pursuant to the provisions of this article and all fines and forfeitures for violations of the provisions of this article that occur on the Durango-La Plata County Airport
premises
and grounds shall be paid into the treasury of the county for the account of the Durango-La Plata County Airport.
(Ord. No. 2014-1, § 3(Exh. A), 4-22-2014)
