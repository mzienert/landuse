Sec. 2-591. Creation.
There is hereby created a solid waste advisory committee.
(Res. No. 1994-12, § 1, 2-1-1994; Res. No. 1997-12, § 1, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 2/10/1997
Sec. 2-592. Composition; appointments.
The solid waste advisory committee
shall
be comprised of seven members appointed by, and serving at the pleasure of, the
board
of
county
commissioners
of La Plata
County
, Colorado.
(Res. No. 1994-12, § 2, 2-1-1994; Res. No. 1995-43, § 1, 10-9-1995; Res. No. 1997-12, § 2, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 2/10/1997
Sec. 2-593. Terms.
The appointed members
shall
serve staggered terms of three years each. Members of the solid waste advisory committee shall serve until their successors are appointed.
(Res. No. 1997-12, §§ 3, 4, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 2/10/1997
Sec. 2-594. Vacancies.
Vacancies on the solid waste advisory committee
shall
be filled by special appointment made by the
board
of
county
commissioners
. Such appointment shall be to fill the unexpired term of the vacant member's seat.
(Res. No. 1997-12, § 5, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 2/10/1997
Sec. 2-595. Qualifications for membership.
All members of the solid waste advisory committee must be residents of the
county
and
shall
have reached the age of 18 years on the effective date of their appointment.
(Res. No. 1997-12, § 6, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 2/10/1997
Sec. 2-596. Officers; meetings; quorum.
I.
The solid waste advisory committee members
shall
elect from their membership a chair, vice-chair and any other officers they deem appropriate for the accomplishment of their task. Meetings of the solid waste advisory committee shall be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the Colorado Sunshine Act, C.R.S. § 24-6-401 et seq. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the solid waste advisory committee shall be posted at the La Plata
County
Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado. The solid waste advisory committee shall meet as often as necessary to fulfill its responsibilities.
II.
The presence of a majority of the solid waste advisory committee shall constitute a quorum for the transaction of business.
(Res. No. 1994-12, §§ 4, 5, 2-1-1994; Res. No. 1997-12, §§ 7, 8, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 10/25/2016
Sec. 2-597. Function; duties.
I.
The solid waste advisory committee
shall
act in an advisory capacity to the
board of county commissioners
.
II.
The duties of the solid waste advisory committee shall be to review the solid waste requirements and opportunities for the
county
; to assist in the preparation of a solid waste
management plan
for the county and an education plan to inform citizens about solid waste matters; and to make advisory recommendations to the board of county commissioners. The solid waste advisory committee shall also review specific matters submitted to it for consideration and perform such other functions as prescribed by the
board
of county
commissioners
.
(Res. No. 1994-12, § 3, 2-1-1994; Res. No. 1997-12, § 9, 2-10-1997)
Effective on: 2/10/1997
