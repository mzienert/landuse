DIVISION 6. TRANSPORT PERMIT OPERATING REQUIREMENTS
Sec. 42-414. Daylight travel requirements.
An extra-legal
vehicle
or load traveling on a
county
highway during daylight hours
shall
comply with the following requirements:
An extra-legal vehicle or load at least 11 feet, but not more than 13 feet in width requires one pilot car in the front.
An extra-legal vehicle or load more than 13 feet, but not more than 15 feet in width requires one pilot car in the front and either one pilot car or one flashing yellow light in the rear.
An extra-legal vehicle or load more than 15 feet in width requires a
special permit
.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(4-9), 9-8-1997)
