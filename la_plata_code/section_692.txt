Sec. 42-416. Overhead structures and utilities.
The
permittee
shall
be responsible for checking all overhead
structures
and
utilities
on the route to be traveled to ensure that the extra-legal
vehicle
or load has adequate clearance of all overheight restrictions. An extra-legal vehicle or load more than 16 feet in height is prohibited from travel unless a pilot car escort vehicle is positioned in the front.
