ARTICLE II. DIRECTIONAL SIGNS AND BUSINESS PLAQUES
Sec. 98-5. Permits required.
A
permit
shall
be required to install a
directional sign
and
business plaque
in the
county
road
right-of-way
or within 30 feet from a county road shoulder. A permit shall be valid for one year, expiring on
May
1 of each year.
