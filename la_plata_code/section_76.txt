Sec. 2-219. Membership; qualifications; application.
The membership of the La Plata County Community Corrections Board shall be by appointment to be made by the appropriate entities involved, as stated in this section following appropriate advertising for such positions. Any interested individuals shall apply in writing, stating in detail the reasons for their applications to serve on the La Plata County Community Corrections Board, and providing pertinent background information in support of their qualifications to serve in that capacity.
