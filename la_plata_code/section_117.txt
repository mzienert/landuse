Sec. 2-362. Membership; terms; appointment; voting.
I.
The membership of the FCARC shall consist of the following:
A.
Chief
of the Los Pinos Fire Protection District (ex officio);
B.
Chief of the Upper Pine Fire Protection District (ex officio);
C.
Chief of the Fort Lewis Mesa Fire Protection District (ex officio);
D.
Chief of the Durango Fire and Rescue Authority (ex officio);
E.
Chair of the
county
building code
board of appeals
(ex officio);
F.
Chair of the county
fire code
board of appeals (ex officio);
G.
Designee of the Home Builders Association of Southwest Colorado;
H.
A Representative of the insurance industry;
I.
Director
of the La Plata County
building
department
(ex officio); and
J.
Plans examiner for the county building department (ex officio; non-voting member).
II.
Appointees shall serve staggered terms of three years each, with the exception of:
A.
The fire chiefs, who serve by virtue of their offices;
B.
The chairs of the La Plata County building code board of appeals and fire code board of appeals, who serve by virtue of their offices; and
C.
The La Plata County building department director and plans examiner, who serve by virtue of their offices.
III.
The representative of the insurance industry shall be appointed by the
board of county commissioners
and shall represent the insurance industry. The designee of the Home Builders Association of Southwest Colorado shall be appointed by the board of county commissioners after being nominated by the Home Builders Association of Southwest Colorado, and shall represent the
construction
industry. The two aforementioned appointees shall be residents of the county and shall have reached the age of 18 years on the effective day of their appointment. The two aforementioned appointees shall continue to serve until their successors are appointed. Vacancies for the two aforementioned appointees shall be filled by special appointment made by the board of county commissioners. Such appointment shall be to fill the unexpired term of the vacant member's seat.
IV.
The plans examiner for the La Plata County building department shall be an ex officio non-voting member. All other members shall be voting members.
(Res. No. 2011-38, § 1(Exh. A), 11-22-2011)
Effective on: 11/22/2011
