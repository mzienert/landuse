Sec. 42-16 CR 501G USE OF OFF-HIGHWAY VEHICLES AND SNOWMOBILES
The Board of County Commissioners hereby designates La Plata County Road 501G as an off-highway vehicle route and snowmobile route thereby permitting the operation of off-highway vehicles and snowmobiles on such road, pursuant to all regulations that may be further set forth herein or that may otherwise be set forth in the Colorado Revised Statutes and regulations promulgated pursuant thereto:
County Road 501G, also known as “Middle Mountain Road,” beginning at the intersection of County Road 501 and extending up Middle Mountain Road one-half (0.5) miles.
The use of the CR 501G for the operation of off-highway vehicles or snowmobiles is subject to the following:
All operations of OHV’s or snowmobiles shall be conducted in a safe manner and in compliance with relevant federal, state and local laws and regulations including, but not limited to, the equipment and registration requirements set forth in  C.R.S. §§ 33-14.5-102, 33-14.5-109, 33-14-102 and 33-14-114.
All operations of OHV’s on CR 501G must comply with the provisions of 2 CCR 405-5, as amended, (Off-Highway Vehicle Regulations) and the remaining provisions of Article 14.5 of Title 33 of the Colorado Revised Statutes related to the operation of off-highway vehicles.
All operations of snowmobiles on CR 501G must comply with the provisions of C.R.S. § 33-14-109, as amended, (Restrictions on Young Operators) and the remaining provisions of Article 14 of Title 33 of the Colorado Revised Statutes related to the operation of snowmobiles.
In accordance with C.R.S. § 33-14.5-110, as amended, all OHV’s shall be operated only by operators who have, in their possession a valid current driver’s license or vehicle operator’s license issued by the State of Colorado, or by any other state.
In accordance with C.R.S. § 33-14.5-110, as amended, all OHV drivers operating on CR 501G shall maintain liability insurance in the minimum amounts set forth in C.R.S. § 10-4-620, as amended.
(Res. No. 2016-11)
Effective on: 10/25/2016
