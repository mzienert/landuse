DIVISION 37. SOUTHWEST LA PLATA LIBRARY DISTRICT
Contents:
Sec. 2-692. Creation; Terms; Vacancies and Qualifications.
Sec. 2-692. Creation; Terms; Vacancies and Qualifications.
I.
Creation. There is hereby established the Southwest La Plata Library District Board of Trustees. The Board of Trustees shall be comprised of seven (7) members appointed by the Board of County Commissioners of La Plata County Colorado.
II.
Term and Vacancies.
A.
Pursuant to C.R.S.
§ 24-90-108(3)(a), the initial terms for the seven-member board of trustees shall be staggered in the following manner:
One-year term - One trustee
Four-year term - Two trustees
Two-year term - One trustee
Five-year term - Two trustees
Three-year term - One trustee
B.
After appointment of the initial trustees for the library district, bylaws adopted by the district shall govern the length and number of terms of the members of the board of trustees, pursuant to C.R.S.
§ 24-90-108(3)(a), as amended.
C.
Unless removed for good cause, members of the board of trustees shall serve until their successors are appointed or
D.
Members of the board of trustees may be removed by the Board of County Commissioners, based upon a showing of good cause as defined in, but not limited to, the bylaws of the library district.
E.
Vacancies shall be filled by special appointment made by the Board of County Commissioners. Such appointments shall be made to fill the unexpired term of the vacant trustee's seat.
F.
The Board authorizes the District's board of trustees to recommend new trustees for consideration for appointment.
G.
The members of the District's board of trustees shall serve without compensation.
III.
Qualifications.
A.
Trustees shall be chosen from the residents within the library district's legal service area.
