Sec. 54-72. Presumption of abandonment.
Motor
vehicles
left unattended on any area of the airport not designated specifically by official signs for the parking of such vehicle, for a period of 24 hours,
shall
be presumed to be abandoned as defined by C.R.S. § 42-4-1802(1)(b) and shall be subject to the public towing provisions of C.R.S. § 42-4-1803(1). Vehicles parked in
violation
of this article in any zone designated and marked by official signs as a tow-away zone
may
be subject to towing and impoundment immediately upon being left unattended by the
owner
or driver of such motor vehicle.
(Ord. No. 2014-1, § 3(Exh. A), 4-22-2014)
Effective on: 4/22/2014
