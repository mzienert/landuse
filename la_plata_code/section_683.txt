Sec. 42-407. Drive in right lane.
An extra-legal
vehicle
or load which is 13 feet or more in width
shall
travel only in the farthest righthand lane available to traffic and shall travel as close as practicable to the righthand curb or painted shoulder line, except when passing another vehicle or preparing for a left turn.
