DIVISION 6. TRANSPORT PERMIT OPERATING REQUIREMENTS
Sec. 42-419. Highway and bridge restrictions.
All oversize and/or overweight
vehicles
or loads are prohibited from travel at any time in the following situations, unless a
special permit
is issued:
If a bridge or highway has been posted with a maximum weight per vehicle, or is listed in the
bridge weight limit map
or the prohibited overweight route list, no vehicle
may
exceed that maximum weight on that bridge or highway even if the vehicle's weight is otherwise permissible by
transport permit
, issued under this article. (See appendices B and C to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which are on file in the clerk and recorder's office.)
Exceptions to the restrictions in this section may be granted if an
emergency
condition warrants movement and if an agreement can be made between the
permittee
and the
department
.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(4-14), 9-8-1997)
