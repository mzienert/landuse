ARTICLE II. STOPPING, STANDING, PARKING
DIVISION 5. PARKING SPACES FOR PERSONS WITH DISABILITIES
Sec. 54-137. Improper use of license plate or placard an offense.
Any
person
who is not a person with a disability and who uses a license plate or placard issued to a person with a disability pursuant to C.R.S. § 42-3-121(2) in order to receive the benefits or privileges available to a person with a disability under this division commits a traffic offense.
(Ord. No. 1997-3, § 6.10-7, 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
