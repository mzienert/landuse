DIVISION 6. TRANSPORT PERMIT OPERATING REQUIREMENTS
Sec. 42-411. Signs required.
An extra-legal
vehicle
or load
shall
display "Wide Load," "Long Load," or "Oversize Load" signs, as applicable, in the extreme front and rear of the extra-legal vehicle or load, except as provided in division 2 of this article. All such signs shall either be five feet wide, ten inches high with one-inch wide brush stroke, black letters a minimum of eight inches high on yellow background; or shall be seven feet wide, 18 inches high, with a 1.41-inch brush stroke, black letters a minimum of ten inches high on yellow background.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(4-6), 9-8-1997)
