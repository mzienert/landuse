DIVISION 3. FEES
Sec. 2-791. Duplicates of microfiche or aperture cards.
Imposition of fees. The
county
clerk and recorder is authorized, empowered, and required to impose the fee in the fee schedule in appendix A of this
Code
for each duplicate microfiche or aperture card produced for the public. Such duplication fees are to be collected by the county clerk and recorder and his authorized deputies and employees at the time such microfiche or aperture card duplicates are produced.
Accounting and depositing. Any revenues which the county clerk and recorder
shall
collect for microfiche and aperture card duplicates as authorized by this section shall be accounted for and deposited in the same manner as other fees collected and received by that office.
(Res. No. 1992-44, §§ 1, 2, 9-29-1992)
Effective on: 9/29/1992
