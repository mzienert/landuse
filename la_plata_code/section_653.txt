DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-282. Security may be required.
Pursuant to authority granted in C.R.S. § 42-4-510(3), when necessary to protect highways from undue damage to the road foundations, surfaces or
structures
, the
owner or operator
under a
special permit
may
be required to post security as may be deemed necessary to compensate for any injury to any highway or highway structure. Security
shall
be posted in a form acceptable to the
director
and may be bond, irrevocable letter of credit or other acceptable security in light of the special circumstances.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(6-11), 9-8-1997)
