DIVISION 24. DURANGO HILLS ROAD IMPROVEMENT ADVISORY COMMITTEE
Sec. 2-683. Powers and duties.
The Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee
shall
act in an advisory capacity and make recommendations to the
board
of
county
commissioners
concerning the annual district budget, work plan and mill levy. The committee shall also be responsible to coordinate and oversee road maintenance and improvement activities and snow removal for those roads within the Durango Hills road improvement district specified in Resolution No. 1997-21.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 9, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
