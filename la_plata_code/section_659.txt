DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-309. Maximum limits annual transport permit.
The
maximum limits
under an annual
transport permit
are as follows:
Sixteen feet in height.
Two hundred thousand pounds gross
vehicle
weight, subject to the maximum limits for axle weight designated on the
bridge weight limit map
.
Seventeen feet in width, subject to the maximum limit for width designated on the bridge weight limit map and prohibited overweight route lists, as provided in appendices D and E to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which are on file in the clerk and recorder's office.
One hundred twenty feet in length for all nonmountainous, two-lane
roadways
.
One hundred ten feet in length for all mountainous two-lane roadways.
Thirty-five-foot overhang.
If an extra-legal vehicle or load operating under an annual
permit
exceeds any one of these maximum limits, the permit automatically becomes void. If an extra-legal vehicle or load exceeds the maximum limits for height or length, the
applicant
must obtain a single-trip transport permit. If an extra-legal vehicle or load exceeds the maximum limits for width or weight, the applicant must obtain a
special permit
.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(3-5), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
