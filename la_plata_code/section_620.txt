DIVISION 2. EXEMPTIONS
Sec. 42-209. Crane boom sections.
Crane boom sections transported side by side as items essential to the operation of the crane
shall
be exempt from section 42-408 regarding divisible loads, provided that such sections do not exceed an overall width of 11 feet, six inches, and provided that the trailer
unit
carrying the crane boom sections travels on the highways with and directly behind the crane. Notwithstanding section 42-412, a distance of not less than 100 feet nor more than 500 feet shall be maintained between the crane and the trailer unit.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(7-9), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
