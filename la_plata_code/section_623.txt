DIVISION 2. EXEMPTIONS
Sec. 42-212. Vehicle crossings.
An extra-legal
vehicle
or load entering
county
highway
right-of-way
for the sole purpose of immediately crossing the county highway, on a one-time basis, is exempt from the requirement to obtain a
permit
if the extra-legal vehicle or load does not exceed the annual permit specifications listed in section
42-309.
The
permittee
of an extra-legal vehicle or load which exceeds the annual permit specifications of section 42-309
shall
contact the
department
prior to crossing the county highway to determine if a permit will be required. In determining whether a permit will be required, the department shall consider the number of proposed crossings, seasonal and weather conditions, time of crossings, protection of efficient movement of traffic, protection of the highway from undue damage to the road, width of the county right-of-way involved and any other factors relevant to the specific right-of-way.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(7-12), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
