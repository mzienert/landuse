Sec. 54-226. Inspection and verification prerequisite to registration and title.
The
county
clerk and recorder is authorized and empowered to employ
persons
to perform inspections and verifications of motor
vehicle
identification numbers as is required by state law as a prerequisite to the clerk's acceptance of applications to register and title motor vehicles in the county.
(Res. No. 1992-43, § 1, 9-22-1992)
Effective on: 9/22/1992
Sec. 54-227. Inspection fee; verification procedures.
The
county
clerk and recorder is authorized, empowered and required to impose a fee as listed in appendix A of this
Code
per motor
vehicle
identification number inspection for each such inspection performed by authorized county employees. Such inspection fees as are imposed hereby are to be collected by the county clerk and recorder and his duly authorized deputies and employees at the time such motor vehicle identification number inspections are performed. In consideration for collection of the required fees, such deputized and authorized county personnel
shall
personally inspect and verify the vehicle identification number or other identifying symbols of each motor vehicle so inspected. Such verification shall include a check of the authenticity of such vehicle identification number or similar identifying symbols with appropriate state and national law enforcement authorities.
(Res. No. 1992-43, § 2, 9-22-1992)
