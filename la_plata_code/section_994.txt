Chapter 62 Title, Effective Date, Purposes and Definitions
Sec. 62-2   Effective Date
The
effective date
of chapters 62 through 68, 70-74 and 79-81 of this code is October 1, 2020. The effective dates of chapter 69 and chapter 78 are October 1, 2019 and August 4, 2014, respectively. The effective date of chapter 90 is August 1, 2023.
(
Res. No.
2020-30
, § 62-2, 09/15/2020; Res. No.
2023-11
, § 90-1, 04/26/2023
)
Effective on: 10/1/2020
