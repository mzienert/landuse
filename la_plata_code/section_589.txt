ARTICLE IV. EXCAVATIONS
DIVISION 2. PERMIT
Sec. 42-143. Specifications.
All work to be performed under a
permit
issued pursuant to this article
shall
be in accordance with the specifications which are on file at the road and bridge
department
and incorporated in this section by reference. If, in the discretion of one of the road and bridge
directors
, or his authorized
agent
, accepted engineering and design principles require the application of additional or different standards, such standards shall govern the performance of the work and shall be set forth in the permit.
(Res. No. 1982-127, § 4, 12-6-1982)
Effective on: 12/6/1982
