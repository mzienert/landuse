DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-276. Road closure notice.
If a condition of the
permit
requires a
county
highway to be closed because the extra-legal
vehicle
or load will
use
the entire highway, the
permittee
shall
provide public notification of the temporary closure of a
state highway
, not less than three weeks in advance of the move. Public notification shall consist of one or more of the following: local newspaper, radio, television or onsite location signs, as determined by the
department
to be appropriate and as indicated by the department in the permit.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(6-5), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
