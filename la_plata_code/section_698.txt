DIVISION 7. PILOT ESCORT VEHICLES
Sec. 42-441. Compliance with traffic law.
When escorting an extra-legal
vehicle
or load, the operator of a pilot car escort vehicle must comply with all applicable traffic laws and requirements of this article in order to protect the safety of highway users and to protect the efficient movement of traffic from unreasonable interference by the extra-legal vehicle or load.
