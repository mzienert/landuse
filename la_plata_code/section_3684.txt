Chapter 100 SPECIAL DISTRICTS
Sec. 100-9. Service plan processing fees.
The fees for processing any service plan are established by state statute. The processing fee
shall
be used to reimburse the
county
for the reasonable direct costs related to processing the service plan and conducting the public hearings on the plan, including but not limited to the costs of notice, publication and recording of testimony. If it is determined that more in depth review of a particular service plan is required, an additional service fee as provided in C.R.S. ยง 32-1-202(3) may be imposed at submittal to reimburse the county for its reasonable direct costs related to the in-depth review.
