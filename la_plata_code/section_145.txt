DIVISION 15. LODGING TAX COMMITTEE
FOOTNOTE(S):
Editor's note—
The lodging tax was adopted by referendum on November 3, 1987, which authorized the creation of a lodging tax committee.
Cross reference—
Financial matters, § 2-741 et seq.; taxation, ch. 50; lodging tax, § 50-91 et seq.
State Law reference—
Countywide lodging tax authorized, C.R.S. § 30-11-107.5.
Sec. 2-463. Duties.
The lodging tax committee
shall
administer the tourism fund created in section 50-99 to advertise and market tourism in the
county
. Where there is an established and proven marketing entity within the county formed for the purpose of advertising and marketing tourism, the committee
may
and is
encouraged
to
use
that entity. The committee or if another entity is used then that entity, shall provide an audited accounting to the
board
of county
commissioners
on an annual basis.
(Res. No. 1987-87, § 10, 9-15-1987)
Effective on: 9/15/1987
