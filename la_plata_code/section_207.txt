DIVISION 24. DURANGO HILLS ROAD IMPROVEMENT ADVISORY COMMITTEE
Sec. 2-681. Meetings.
Meetings of the Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee
shall
be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the provisions of C.R.S. 24-6-402 and C.R.S. 30-20-511. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the Durango Hills road improvement advisory committee shall be posted at three public places within the limits of the district and the La Plata
County
Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado, at least three days previous to any such meeting. The Durango Hills road improvement advisory committee shall meet as often as necessary to fulfill its responsibilities.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 7, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 10/25/2016
