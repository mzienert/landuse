Sec. 2-516. Created.
In accordance with a petition filed with the
board
of
county
commissioners
, a cemetery district to be known as the Pine River Cemetery District, within the boundaries of the
county
, as described and on file in the county clerk and recorder's office is declared created.
(Res. of 10-13-1953)
Effective on: 10/13/1953
Sec. 2-517. Membership and terms.
I.
The Pine River Cemetery District committee
shall
consist of five members appointed by the
board
of
county
commissioners
for staggered terms.
II.
The terms of the members shall be six years.
III.
The members shall reside within the boundaries of the Pine River Cemetery District.
Effective on: 10/13/1953
Sec. 2-518. Meetings.
The Pine River Cemetery District committee
shall
meet upon the call of the chair when such meeting is necessary.
Effective on: 10/13/1953
Sec. 2-519. Duties.
The Pine River Cemetery District committee
shall
oversee and administer the operation of the Pine River Cemetery.
Effective on: 10/13/1953
