Sec. 2-119. Modifications.
The city, the
county
and the director of aviation are parties to an employment agreement for the director of aviation. A committee consisting of the city manager, county manager and chairperson of the airport commission
shall
have the authority to make routine amendments or modifications to the employment contract of the director of aviation; provided, however, that no such amendments shall occur without the consent of both the city manager and the county manager. Such modifications or amendments
may
be based upon recommendations of the airport commission. If either the city manager or the county manager disagrees with one another or the recommendations of the airport commission, or any other recommendations related to the employment of the director of aviation, then the matter shall be referred to the board of county commissioners and the Durango city council for determination, along with the recommendations of the airport commission, the county manager, the city manager, and the chairperson of the airport commission.
(Res. No. 2002-58, § 9, 12-9-2002; Res. No. 2013-12, § 10, 3-12-2013)
