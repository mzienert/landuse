DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-246. Transfer of annual permits.
Annual
transport permits
may
be transferred to another extra-legal
vehicle
or load or replaced for the remainder of the
permit
term only upon compliance with the following conditions:
The
applicant
for a permit transfer or replacement must submit a written application to the
department
describing in detail the reasons for the transfer or replacement.
A permit which is transferred or replaced
shall
be issued only to the original
permittee
.
The applicant for a permit transfer or replacement shall submit a fee as listed in appendix A of this
Code
.
The request for a permit transfer must be accompanied by the original transport permit.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(3-9), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
