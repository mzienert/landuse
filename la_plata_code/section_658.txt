DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-308. Terms and conditions.
Upon issuance of a
transport permit
, the
permittee
agrees to abide by the terms and conditions that the
department
places on such
permit
, pursuant to this article. Applications for transport permits for a
vehicle
having any axle or gross load weight exceeding the legal limit
may
be required to furnish with the application weight tickets from a certified scale on each axle or group of axles of the vehicle, a copy of the vehicle registration, and a letter verifying any additional weight that has been added to the original vehicle.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(3-1), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
