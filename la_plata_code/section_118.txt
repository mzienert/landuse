Sec. 2-363. Officers; meetings.
The FCARC
shall
elect from its membership annually a chair, vice-chair, and any other officers it deems appropriate for the accomplishment of its duties. The plans examiner for the La Plata
County
building
department
shall serve as secretary to the FCARC and shall keep records of all official
actions
and minutes of the FCARC's proceedings showing the vote of each member upon each question, or if absent or failing to vote, indicating such a fact. All such records and minutes shall be public records. Meetings of the FCARC shall be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the Colorado Sunshine Act, C.R.S. § 24-6-401 et seq. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the FCARC shall be posted at the La Plata County Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado. Pursuant to chapter 34 of this
Code
, the FCARC shall, at a minimum, meet biannually at a time and place of its choosing. The FCARC
may
adopt supplemental rules of procedure not inconsistent with Colorado law or this Code.
(Res. No. 2011-38, § 1(Exh. A), 11-22-2011)
Effective on: 10/25/2016
