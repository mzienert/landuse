Chapter 18 BUILDINGS AND BUILDING REGULATIONS
Article III. Building Codes and standards: Amendments, additions and deletions to the international codes
Sec. 18-38 Amendments and Deletions to the 2015 International Building Code
As provided above in Section
18-35
, Chapter 1 of the 2015 International Building Code is deleted in its entirety and replaced by Section
18-35
of this Code. Otherwise, the 2015 International Building Code is adopted excepted as specified in this section.
Section 3109
Swimming Pool Enclosures and Safety Devices
Section 3109.4 Residential swimming pools. Section 3109.4 of the 2015 International Building Code is deleted in its entirety and replaced with the following:
Swimming pools, spas, hot tubs, wading pools, etc. associated with single family dwellings are exempt from Section 3109. Community swimming pools, spas, hot tubs, wading pools etc. are subject to all the provisions of Section 3109.
Effective on: 12/11/2017
