ARTICLE I. IN GENERAL
Sec. 42-1. Adoption of Public Improvement Inspection Manual; copies available to public.
The Public
Improvement
Inspection Manual for the
county
road and bridge
department
, incorporated in this section by reference is hereby adopted. The provisions of the manual
shall
be applicable to all
persons
, corporations, or entities engaged in any
construction
or other activities within the rights-of-way of the county road system.
Copies of the Public Improvement Inspection Manual for the county road and bridge department shall be available to the public at the county road and bridge department, the county clerk and recorder's office, and the office of the
board of county commissioners
at a price to be set by the board of county commissioners.
(Res. No. 1985-99, 8-27-1985)
Effective on: 8/27/1985
