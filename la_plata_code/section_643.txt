DIVISION 3. PERMITS
Sec. 42-272. General requirements.
An extra-legal
vehicle
or load which the
department
determines, based upon the application information, either requires extraordinary
action
or exceeds the applicable limits, and which does not qualify for a state special mobile machinery exemption, is prohibited from travel unless authorized by a
special permit
.
An extra-legal vehicle or load under a special permit
shall
comply with the following:
Applicable requirements of this article.
All conditions of the
permit
, which the department determined to be necessary after a detailed analysis of the extra-legal vehicle or load and the move including, but not limited to:
The size and type of load to be moved.
The method to be utilized in making the move.
The areas where the move originates and terminates.
Route requested for the move.
The distance of the move.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(6-1), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
