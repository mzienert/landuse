Sec. 54-84. Designation.
Parking
control
zones
shall
be designated by signs in accordance with the Manual of Uniform Traffic Control Devices.
The
director
of aviation or the road maintenance director shall designate parking control zones in writing and submit such zones to the
board of county commissioners
.
Such zones shall be deemed effective unless the board of county commissioners, within ten days of receipt, rescinds or revises such zones. The director of aviation or road maintenance director shall cause a record to be kept of all zones designated by incorporation of such records into the
commissioners
' minutes.
The designated zones shall be effective until subsequently revised by designation by the director of aviation or the road maintenance director, or by order of the board of county commissioners as reflected in the minutes.
(Ord. No. 2014-1, § 3(Exh. A), 4-22-2014)
