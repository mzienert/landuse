Sec. 2-676. Creation.
That there is hereby created the Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee to be comprised of seven members appointed by, and serving at the pleasure of, the
board
of
county
commissioners
.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 1, 6-8-1998; Res. No. 2012-11, § 1, 2-14-2012)
Effective on: 2/14/2012
Sec. 2-677. Terms.
Appointees
shall
serve staggered terms of three years each. Members of the Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee shall serve until their successors are appointed.
(Res. No. 1998-22, §§ 2, 3, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
Sec. 2-678. Vacancies.
Vacancies
shall
be filled by special appointment made by the
board
of
county
commissioners
. Such appointment shall be to fill the unexpired term of the vacant member's seat.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 4, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
Sec. 2-679. Qualifications of members.
All members must be residents of the district and
shall
have reached the age of 18 years on the effective date of their appointment.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 5, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
Sec. 2-680. Officers.
The Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee members
shall
elect from their membership a chair, vice-chair, and any other officers they deem appropriate for the accomplishment of their tasks.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 6, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
Sec. 2-681. Meetings.
Meetings of the Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee
shall
be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the provisions of C.R.S. 24-6-402 and C.R.S. 30-20-511. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the Durango Hills road improvement advisory committee shall be posted at three public places within the limits of the district and the La Plata
County
Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado, at least three days previous to any such meeting. The Durango Hills road improvement advisory committee shall meet as often as necessary to fulfill its responsibilities.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 7, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 10/25/2016
Sec. 2-682. Quorum.
The presence of a majority of the Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee
shall
constitute a quorum for the transaction of business.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 8, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
Sec. 2-683. Powers and duties.
The Durango Hills road
improvement
advisory committee
shall
act in an advisory capacity and make recommendations to the
board
of
county
commissioners
concerning the annual district budget, work plan and mill levy. The committee shall also be responsible to coordinate and oversee road maintenance and improvement activities and snow removal for those roads within the Durango Hills road improvement district specified in Resolution No. 1997-21.
(Res. No. 1998-22, § 9, 6-8-1998)
Effective on: 6/8/1998
Secs. 2-684, 2-685. Reserved.
Effective on: 6/8/1998
