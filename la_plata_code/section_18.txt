ARTICLE I. IN GENERAL
Sec. 2-3. Adoption of the National Incident Management System.
The
county
hereby adopts the NIMS standardized procedures for managing personnel, communications, facilities and resources as the county standard for its incident response or preparedness needs.
Every public official in this county who is responsible for incident response or preparedness on behalf of the county
shall
adhere to the above policy and act as soon as possible to implement such procedures.
(Res. No. 2006-14, §§ 1, 2, 3-28-2006)
Editor's note—
Res. No. 2006-14, §§ 1, 2, adopted March 28, 2006, did not specify manner of inclusion; hence, inclusion as § 2-3 is at the discretion of the editor.
