ARTICLE IV. EXCAVATIONS
DIVISION 2. PERMIT
Sec. 42-141. Required.
No
person
shall
excavate, trench, dig or otherwise disturb, in any way whatsoever, any
public highway
right-of-way
for the purpose of constructing, installing or repairing any
utility
line, without first having obtained a
permit
so to do as set forth in this division.
Permits shall be obtained before work begins, except that, in cases of
emergency
, permits shall be obtained as soon as practical after work begins, but in no event later than the next working day. The sheriff's
department
shall be notified when emergency work is begun before a permit is obtained.
Permits shall be issued by the appropriate official from the
county
road and bridge department or his authorized
agent
. Permits shall be available for inspection at the
project
site
at all times.
(Res. No. 1982-127, § 2, 12-6-1982; Res. No. 2003-08, § 2, 2-18-2003)
Effective on: 12/6/1982
