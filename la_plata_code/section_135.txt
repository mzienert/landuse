Sec. 2-436. District created.
I.
The
board
of
county
commissioners
of La Plata
County
, in cooperation with the Town of Ignacio, hereby establishes the Ignacio
Community
Library District pursuant to C.R.S. § 24-90-101 et seq.
II.
The service area of the library district
shall
be all of the area included in the Ignacio District 11 Joint R located in La Plata County, Colorado. A complete description of the area to be included in the service area of the library district is on file in the county clerk and recorder's office which is incorporated in this section by this reference.
III.
A mill levy of up to 1.0 mills, as provided in C.R.S. § 24-90-112(1)(a)(II), shall be imposed to fund the operations of the library district.
(Res. No. 1991-4, §§ 1—3, 2-19-1991)
State Law reference—
Library districts, C.R.S. § 24-90-110.5.
Effective on: 2/19/1991
Sec. 2-437. Name of library.
The library operated by the library district
shall
be dedicated to the memory of Butch McClanahan and shall be called "The Butch McClanahan Memorial Library."
(Res. No. 1991-4, § 4, 2-19-1991)
Effective on: 2/19/1991
Sec. 2-438. Membership and terms.
The Ignacio
Community
Library
Board
of Trustees
shall
consist of five members appointed by the committee appointed by the Town of Ignacio and the
board of county commissioners
pursuant to C.R.S. § 24-90-108(2)(c). The terms of the members shall be five years. The members appointed shall reside within the boundaries of the Ignacio Community Library District.
State Law reference—
Membership, C.R.S. § 24-90-108(1).
Effective on: 2/19/1991
Sec. 2-439. Duties.
The Ignacio
Community
Library
Board
of Trustees
shall
oversee the operation of the Butch McClanahan Memorial Library.
State Law reference—
Powers and duties, C.R.S. § 24-90-109.
Effective on: 2/19/1991
Sec. 2-440. Meetings.
The Ignacio
Community
Library
Board
of Trustees
shall
meet once every other month as needed.
Effective on: 2/19/1991
