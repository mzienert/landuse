DIVISION 3. WEED MANAGEMENT PLAN
Sec.   58-101 Weed Management Plan
This Plan shall be known and referred to as the "La Plata County Weed Management and Enforcement Plan" and shall be effective throughout the unincorporated areas of La Plata County.
