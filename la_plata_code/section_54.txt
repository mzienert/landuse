Sec. 2-117. Meetings.
Meetings of the airport commission shall be open to the public and conducted in accordance with the C.R.S. §§ 24-72-201 et seq. Notices and agendas of public meetings of the airport commission shall be posted at the La Plata County Administration Building located at 1101 East Second Avenue, Durango, Colorado and Durango City Hall, 949 E. 2nd Ave., Durango, Colorado. The airport commission shall meet as often as necessary to fulfill its responsibilities.
