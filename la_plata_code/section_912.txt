Sec. 58-70. Recoverable costs/method of collection—Public lands.
Any expenses incurred by the
BOCC
in the undertaking of the eradication or
management
of
noxious weeds
on public lands
shall
be a proper charge against such state
board
,
department
, or agency that has
jurisdiction
over the lands.
An agreement for the reimbursement of such expenses shall be reached within two weeks after the date such an expense is submitted to such state board, department or agency, such agreement to be set forth in writing.
If an agreement is not reached or the charge is not immediately paid, such charge shall be submitted to the controller, who shall treat such amount as an encumbrance on the budget of the state board, department or agency involved or such charge
may
be recovered in any court with jurisdiction over such lands.
(Res. No. 2006-43, § 1(Annex A), 11-27-2006)
Effective on: 11/27/2006
