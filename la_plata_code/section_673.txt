Sec. 42-381. Prohibited overweight route list.
Vehicles
which exceed applicable weight limits are prohibited from travel on those
roadways
listed in appendix C to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which is on file in the clerk and recorder's office, except as provided in this division.
Special permits
may
be issued for travel on these prohibited overweight routes when no reasonable alternative route is feasible.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(10-1), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Sec. 42-382. Designated route list.
Vehicles
which exceed applicable weight limits
shall
utilize the designated routes listed in appendix D to Ordinance No. 1997-1 whenever feasible. The requirement set forth in this section shall be a condition applicable to all
permits
.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(10-2), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Sec. 42-383. Posting of county specific limits.
Upon adoption of
county
specific weight limits, which
may
vary from the weight limits applicable to
state highways
and bridges pursuant to the terms of this article, the road and bridge
department
shall
post the roads and bridges, subject to conformity with the state manual and specifications issued pursuant to C.R.S. § 42-4-104, as amended.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(10-3), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Sec. 42-384. County road and bridge limits; prohibiting truck traffic.
The
board
of
county
commissioners
may
set weight limits for any
county
road or bridge by resolution or amendment hereto duly adopted, and may prohibit truck traffic on any county road or bridge. Such resolution or amendment may specify such weight limits by empty weight, gross
vehicle
weight or gross vehicle weight rating, and may specify different weight limits based upon number of axles or vehicle configuration, or on any other basis authorized or utilized by this article. The resolutions or amendments authorized in this section
shall
become respectively appendices E and F to Ordinance No. 1997-1, which shall be on file in the clerk and recorder's office as if set forth fully in this section.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(10-4), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
Sec. 42-385. Temporary county road and bridge limits.
The
director
of the road and bridge
department
, or his designee, is hereby authorized to establish temporary weight limits and to order the posting of such limits in compliance with the law. Such weight limits
shall
be in effect only until the next regular meeting of the
board of county commissioners
, or 30 days, whichever is greater, at which time such weight limits shall be adopted or shall be deemed rescinded by the failure of the
board
of
county
commissioners
to adopt such limits.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(10-5), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
