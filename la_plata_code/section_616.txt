DIVISION 2. EXEMPTIONS
Sec. 42-205. Commercial snow removal vehicles.
Commercial snow removal
vehicles
that are within all
legal limits
except width
shall
be exempt from the travel time restrictions of section 42-406, the sign requirements of section 42-411, and the pilot car escort vehicle requirements of section 42-441, subject to the following conditions:
The vehicle must not exceed 12 feet in width; and
The snow removal blade must be raised and turned parallel to the highway as much as possible, and when raised and turned, shall not exceed 12 feet in width with respect to the highway, at all times when the vehicle is moving on a
county
highway but not plowing snow, so that the width of the blade to oncoming traffic is minimized.
(Ord. No. 1997-1, § 3(7-5), 9-8-1997)
Effective on: 9/8/1997
